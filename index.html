Equivalent Exchange in the Radioactive Deep

A casual mobile arcade-roguelite where the ocean mutates against you.

The Setting: Atomic Abyss

An atomic bomb sits at the bottom of the ocean, leaking radioactive gas that mutates deep-sea wildlife. As a specialized diver, you must capture these threats before they reach the surface.

Core Game Loop

DESCEND
Navigate mines & radiation
CAPTURE
Engage mutations for rewards
UPGRADE
Modify gear & oxygen
REPEAT
Dive deeper for boss fights

Technical & Art Direction

Inspired by the simplicity of Buckshot Roulette, our systems prioritize high-impact mechanics and "silly, buoyant" submerged audio.

  • Modular Spawning: Biomes (Shallow, Midnight, Abyss) triggered by Y-coordinate.
  • Object Pooling: Recycles off-screen assets to prevent battery drain on mobile devices.
  • Mutation RNG: Procedural variation ensures no two dives are identical.
