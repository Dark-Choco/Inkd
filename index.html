INK'D

The deeper you hunt, the more the ocean mutates against you.

A casual mobile arcade-roguelite designed for simple, offline play in your spare time.

The Setting: Radioactive Deep

An atomic bomb rests at the bottom of a deep ocean, leaking radioactive gas that causes abnormal wildlife mutations. Specialized divers are sent to neutralize these mutated threats before they reach the land.

Core Game Loop

DESCEND
Navigate mines and radiation zones.
ENGAGE
Avoid poisonous ink and capture mutated squids.
UPGRADE
Spend earnings on oxygen, weapons, and mobility.
REPEAT
Dive deeper for rarer squids and boss rewards.

Technical Infrastructure & Feasibility

As the technical foundation for our vertical descent arcade-roguelite, we have prioritized mobile performance and modular scalability.

  • Modular Spawning System: Divides the ocean into depth biomes (Shallow, Midnight, Abyss) with Y-coordinate triggers for balanced distribution.
  • Performance Optimization: Uses Object Pooling to recycle off-screen assets, preventing battery drain and lag on older devices.
  • Mutation RNG: A unique procedural system ensures no two dives are identical, driving high replayability.

Art Direction & UX

Our visuals are inspired by popular minimalistic 2D game art, featuring 2D animated skeletons and modular equipment sets.

Audio Experience: A buoyant, submerged atmosphere with "silly" sound bytes to keep the casual tone consistent.
